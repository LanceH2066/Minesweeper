How To Play

Reveal Tiles

  • Left click a tile to reveal what's underneath.
  • If the tile is empty, it will uncover adjacent tiles until numbers or edges are reached.
  • If you click a mine, you lose!

  • Right-click to place a flag on a tile you suspect contains a mine.
  • Right-click again to remove the flag.

Number On Tiles

  • A number on a tile indicates how many mines are adjacent to it.
  • Use these numbers to figure out where the mines are hidden.

Winning The Game

  • Successfully mark all the mines and uncover all the safe tiles to win the game!
